About Us

This club is a place where students find the latest information about topics related to Internet of Things. Students of the Iotronics Club will be offered various programs and activities such as seminars , workshops , short courses , and many as listed below. This club is supported by internal and external experts who assisted the students in strategizing and organizing activities to help them , gain knowledge and skills related to Iot . This club aims to guide students and prepare them to pursue their proffesional careers in Industries especially based on Iot.
